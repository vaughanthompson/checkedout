Your ROI (Return on Investment)

Selling your property? Find out how CheckedOut and our MSIP Building Reports can deliver you massive benefits.
 
 
 
  • You could end up not paying a cent for your MSIP inspection - it can be FREE!
  • Buyers purchase and download your MSIP report immediately and can rely on the report.
  • Buyers have trusted, independent information and are more likely to make an offer.
  • Buyers can decide faster because they have the reliable information faster.
  • Less conditions of sale required on your S&P agreement, faster settlement, and no surprises in negotiations- resulting in the sale price you know you deserve.

Potential Buyers of your home will reward you...

By sharing report costs, making offers and creating competition for your property. PLUS:
  • You and your buyers know your home's true condition, accessing the same information.
  • You have peace of mind knowing the buyers report won't come back with some unexpected issues.
  • Both you and the buyer will be negotiating from the same MSIP report- the level playing field creates a smoother sales process.
  • Buyers can check out your home quickly and seriously- able to rely on the MSIP report you've commissioned.
  • The MSIP inspection model shares the cost of the MSIP inspection with potential buyers, so everyone pays a discounted MSIP report cost.
  • Goodwill. Buyers will appreciate the commitment to an MSIP inspection, the transparency will contibute to a positive negotiation.

CheckedOut provides Payment Options for ALL situations - and how about a 100% ROI?

  • Pay upfront or pay only the balance in 30 days less any refunds you receive.
  • If there is enough interest in your home, you may not pay a cent for the inspection. It can be FREE!
  • Our sharing model (where buyers contribute to the cost of the MSIP Inspection report) means you are rewarded for providing this independent MSIP for the buyers.
  • Buyers acknowledge your effort and transparency by providing this MSIP report of your home and are happy to contribute towards the cost of the MSIP inspection, saving them a lot of time and money NOT having to book their own building inspection!

Negotiate with the 'One Report'

By negotiating with the same report (your MSIP building report) this means you, your agent and the homebuyer all have the same information when negotiating the sale of your home.

Prevent costly re-negotiations

Before CheckedOut's 'One Report' option, the old model forced individual homebuyers to order their own building inspection. When new or contradictory issues were identified by these different reports, it triggered unplanned and costly re-negotiations for home sellers.

But with everyone using the same information from the 'One report'...

  • there are no surprises for the seller prior to sale
  • there is 100% transparency identifying any issue
  • there is no risk of time off the market for the seller
  • there is no risk of the seller being forced to lower their price.
