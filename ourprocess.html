Why do I need MSIP, and what is it?

MSIP provides you with the trusted and reliable information you need, for the biggest purchase in your life:

  • before you decide to make an offer and
  • before you sign a legally binding contract

MSIP is a transparent process that creates a trusted building report unlike the traditional standard vendor's building report. So now, there's only 1 report everyone needs.

A CheckedOut MSIP report provides peace of mind, saves you money, saves you time, and provides you with the confidence in the report information so you can make the best decisions before you place an offer and before you sign.
