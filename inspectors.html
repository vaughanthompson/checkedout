Inspectors

To complete MSIP inspection for CheckedOut all inspectors first have to be 'checked out'.

 
 
  • Only experienced and suitably qualified inspectors are used
  • All have current Professional Indemnity Insurance
  • Are all contracted to complete MSIP inspections
  • CheckedOut complete annual reviews of each inspector

Inspection Costs

All inspections ordered through CheckedOut are always charged out at the standard area base rate or discounted.


Feedback systems

CheckedOut Inspectors will continue to be checked for consistent high standards through our inspector rating & feedback system. Customers will be asked to rate their inspector to ensure our quality service.
