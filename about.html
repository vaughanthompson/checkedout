About Us

CheckedOut are a couple of kiwis with a genuine
interest in the Real Estate industry.
 
 
 

If it's broke...

Our goal is to provide homebuyers with trustworthy, cost effective Due Diligence before they make that life changing decision.

From individual experiences we all had as homebuyers, some of the processes within the sector just made no sense to us and appeared to be backward, slow and costly.

Not being Agents, Inspectors, or Insurers, we have a unique, empathetic perspective of how everything works within the industry from the point of view of everyday sellers and buyers.

We want to ensure everyone in the sales process benefits, not just one side- that's the sharing economy we want to be part of.

...fix it!

Why CheckedOut?

The name CheckedOut just made sense because that's what we want to help everyone do; check everything out before wasting money AND ensure they don't rush into making decisions they regret.

The current sales model still has the majority of homebuyers making offers on a property first and then doing their due diligence to make sure the property has no issues once you have already signed a legally binding Sale & Purchase agreement.

You don't buy anything else that way. It made no sense.

FOMO has caused everyone to rush the sales process and take risks, so we wanted to find a quick, but still trusted way to provide potential buyers with information they could trust & rely on before making one of life's biggest decisions.

 

Take buying a car as an example.

Having an Independent AA Vehicle inspection performed before the purchase is not only a great example of why getting it checked out is so important, but it's also common practice- expected by both buyers and sellers.

For a small fee you have peace of mind knowing the vehicle you are about to spend thousands on is in fact mechanically sound and okay.

So why not demand the same for a property worth hundreds of thousands of dollars, if not more?

CheckedOut was born.
