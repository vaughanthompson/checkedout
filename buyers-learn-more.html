Book a MSIP Inspection NOW

Building reports are one of the 2 main causes of a property contract falling over and collapsing in NZ.

By the time a sale does collapse, you could have spent thousands of dollars and countless hours arranging everything from a building inspection, LIM, insurance, finance and a property valuation. All you have to show for your efforts are numerous documents you no longer need or want.

The building report usually ends up in the rubbish or gathering cobwebs.

This is exactly why we created CheckedOut, to help reduce the time and money spent procuring property due diligence.

Homebuyers who missed out on a property can now share their unwanted MSIP reports with other potential buyers who may be interested in this same property, thereby preventing other buyers ordering more of the same due diligence you already have and no longer need or want.

If another buyer purchases your report and goes on to purchase the property, they'll agree to refund you the cost of your inspection, in return CheckedOut arranges the transfer of the reports ownership into the new homeowners name for insurance purposes.

How do homebuyers know they can 'trust' a MSIP report is independent, impartial and not a report prepared by a cowboy builder or a builder friend of the seller?

We ONLY allow building reports that have been completed in accordance with MSIP to be shared and sold on CheckedOut. This provides an assurance for any home buyers that the information has been verified as impartial, independent and is without bias.

By having the property inspected through CheckedOut's MSIP process you give yourself the best chance of reselling your report should you miss out on the property for any reason.

Whether you're In the conditional period of a S&P contract or going to an Auction or Deadline sale, MSIP Inspections are available for every type of property sale method.

Our MSIP service is FREE?

CheckedOut doesn't charge homebuyers for the MSIP service, it's FREE. Buyers only pay for the building inspection fee.


What does CheckedOut charge and when?

Only IF you need to resell your report on CheckedOut will you be charged a one off fee of $49 +gst.

PLUS ALL building inspection prices are passed through CheckedOut to homebuyers without any additional surcharge, fees OR margins.
